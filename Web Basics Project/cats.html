Tips for Bringing a Cat Home

Here is what to keep in mind when you first bring your cat home:

  • Give them time to adjust
    • Most cats need 1 to 3 weeks to feel comfortable
    • Start them in one quiet room first
    • Let the cat come to you, do not force it
  • Cover the basics
    • Fresh water and food every day
    • A clean litter box at all times
    • A scratching post so they do not scratch your furniture
  • Take care of their health
    • See a vet within the first two weeks
    • Keep up with yearly vaccines and flea treatment
