How to Adopt

Adopting from Pawside Haven is easy. Just follow these four steps:

  • Step 1: Browse our available cats online or visit us in person.
  • Step 2: Meet a cat in one of our visit rooms.
  • Step 3: Fill out a short adoption application.
  • Step 4: Take your new cat home the same day!

Requirements

To adopt, you must:

  • Be 18 years old or older
  • Show a valid photo ID
  • Have landlord permission if you rent
  • Agree to keep the cat indoors
  • Sign an agreement not to declaw
  • Visit a vet within 30 days of adopting
